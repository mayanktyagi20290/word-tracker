About SEOTriggers

Last updated: May 2026

SEOTriggers is a free, browser-based SEO content optimization tool built to help writers, bloggers, students, and marketers create content that actually ranks in search engines. Our goal is simple: take the guesswork out of on-page SEO and put professional-grade content analysis in everyone's hands, at no cost.

Why we built it

Most quality SEO content tools are expensive, locked behind subscriptions, and require you to upload your work to a server before you can see any feedback. We wanted to do the opposite. SEOTriggers runs entirely in your browser — your content is analyzed locally on your own device and is never uploaded, stored, or shared. That makes it safe for unpublished drafts, client work, and confidential material.

What SEOTriggers does

Paste any draft and SEOTriggers instantly analyzes it across more than ten dimensions of on-page SEO, including:

  • Word and character counts, reading and speaking time
  • Keyword frequency and density, with overuse and stuffing warnings
  • Long-tail keyword and phrase detection
  • WH question extraction for featured-snippet opportunities
  • NLP entity analysis and topic clustering
  • Search intent classification
  • EEAT (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust) signals
  • Competitor gap analysis
  • A single weighted SEO score out of 100

Who it's for

SEOTriggers is for anyone who publishes online — content writers refining drafts, SEO specialists running quick on-page audits, students improving essays and articles, and small businesses or agencies that want a fast, private, no-cost alternative to premium content suites.

Our commitment

We are committed to keeping the core tool free, fast, and private. We continually refine our analysis based on established SEO best practices and search engine guidance. SEOTriggers provides educational guidance and estimates; it does not guarantee rankings, and search results depend on many factors outside any single tool's control.
