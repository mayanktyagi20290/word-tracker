Keyword density thresholds we use

There's no single official rule, but these bands reflect widely-used SEO guidance.

DensityWhat it usually means
Under 0.5%Low — the keyword may be under-represented relative to the page's apparent topic.
0.5% – 3%Good — a natural, commonly recommended range for a primary keyword.
3% – 5%Warning — starting to look repetitive; worth a read-through for natural flow.
Above 5%Stuffing risk — likely to read unnaturally and may draw attention from quality-focused algorithms.
