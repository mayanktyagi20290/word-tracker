Why convert JPG to PNG?

PNG is lossless and supports sharp edges and text cleanly, which makes it the better choice for logos, screenshots, diagrams and any graphic you plan to edit repeatedly — re-saving a JPG each time slowly degrades it, while PNG does not. PNG also supports transparency, although a JPG has no transparency to carry over.

Frequently asked questions

How do I convert JPG to PNG?

Drop your JPG or JPEG files onto the box above. Each one is converted to PNG in your browser and ready to download in a second, individually or all at once.

Is converting JPG to PNG lossless?

The conversion to PNG is lossless, but it cannot restore detail already lost when the image was first saved as JPG. The PNG will look identical to your JPG and won't degrade further on re-saving.

Will the PNG have a transparent background?

No. A JPG has no transparency, so the converted PNG has the same solid background. PNG simply preserves it without any quality loss.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. Conversion happens entirely in your browser, so your images never leave your device — faster and more private than tools that upload your files.

Can I convert many images at once?
