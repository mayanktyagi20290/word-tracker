Editorial Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At SEOTriggers, we want every guide, article, and tooltip on the Site to be accurate, useful, and trustworthy. This Editorial Policy explains how we create and maintain our content.

Accuracy and research

Our content is based on established SEO best practices, official search engine documentation, and widely accepted industry guidance. We aim to explain concepts clearly and honestly, including the limitations of what automated analysis can and cannot tell you.

Review and editing

Articles are reviewed for accuracy and clarity before publishing. When search engine guidance or best practices change, we revisit relevant content and update it. Each article shows a "last updated" date so you know how current it is.

Corrections

We're committed to correcting mistakes. If you spot an error or something out of date, please tell us at support@seotriggers.com and we'll review and fix it promptly.

Honesty about our tools

We describe our scores and metrics as what they are — heuristic estimates meant to guide editing decisions, not guarantees of rankings or definitive verdicts. We will not overstate what the tool can do.

Editorial independence from advertising

SEOTriggers may display advertising (including through Google AdSense) to keep the tool free. Advertising and any commercial relationships do not influence our educational content, our analysis methodology, or the suggestions our tool provides. Ads are clearly distinguishable from editorial content.

Affiliate and sponsored content

If we ever publish affiliate links or sponsored content, we will clearly disclose it. We only recommend things we believe provide genuine value to our users.

Questions about our editorial standards? Reach us at support@seotriggers.com.
