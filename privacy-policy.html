Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SEOTriggers ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you visit seotriggers.com (the "Site") and use our tools. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described here.

1. The content you analyze stays in your browser

SEOTriggers performs all content analysis locally, in your web browser, using JavaScript. The text you paste or type into our tool is never uploaded to, transmitted to, or stored on our servers. We do not see, collect, or retain the content you analyze. When you close or refresh the page, that content is gone unless your browser locally saves it for your own convenience.

2. Information we collect

Because the tool runs client-side, we collect very little. We may collect:

  • Standard log and analytics data — such as anonymized page views, browser type, device type, approximate region, and referring pages, used to understand traffic and improve the Site.
  • Information you voluntarily provide — for example, your email address and message if you contact us.

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the tool.

3. Cookies and similar technologies

The Site may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, measure traffic through analytics, and (where applicable) serve advertising. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function as intended.

4. Advertising and third-party vendors (Google AdSense)

We may display advertising served by third-party vendors, including Google, through Google AdSense. These vendors use cookies and similar technologies to serve and personalize ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our Site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or opt out of third-party vendor cookies at www.aboutads.info/choices.
  • For more information on how Google uses data, see How Google uses information from sites that use its services.

5. Analytics

We may use privacy-conscious analytics tools to measure aggregate, anonymized usage. These tools may set cookies or collect standard technical data as described above. They do not receive the content you analyze in the tool.

6. Your rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data, and to opt out of certain data uses. If you are in the EEA/UK (GDPR) or California (CCPA/CPRA), you may exercise these rights by contacting us at support@seotriggers.com. Because we collect minimal personal data and never receive your analyzed content, most requests relate only to contact correspondence and standard analytics.

7. Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited information we handle. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page, and we will update the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@seotriggers.com or visit our Contact page.
